Hércules : Hércules SGI indicators

Created by Juan Tardón Martínez on feb 08, 2022

The functional analysis of the Hercules SGI (CRIS) has a section for Business Intelligence - Indicators that has been used to extract the SGI metrics and align them within the Snowball metrics and framework.

The sections of the Hércules Indicators analysis are (in Spanish):

  • Indicadores de transferencia de conocimiento e innovación (SICTI). SICTI stands for Spanish System for Science, Technology and Innovation.
    • REP-BI-0010- Patentes
    • REP-BI-0020-EBTs
    • REP-BI-0030-Contratos
    • REP-BI-040- Oferta tecnológica
  • Indicadores CRUE. CRUE stands for Conference of Principals of Spanish Universities.
    • REP-BI-0050- RRHH
    • REP-BI-0060- Ingresos
    • REP-BI-0070- Proyectos y contratos
    • REP-BI-0080- Startup
    • REP-BI-0090- Sexenios
    • REP-BI-00100- Investigadores
    • REP-BI-00110- PRC
  • SIIU (Recursos humanos). SIIU stands for Integrated University Information System. The analysis section holds indicators from the Human Resource area.
    • REP-BI-0130- Matriculación
  • OTRI. OTRI stands for Technology Transfer Office from the University of Murcia.
  • UGI. UGI stands for Research Management Unit from the University of Murcia.
